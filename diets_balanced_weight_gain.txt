
Balanced Calorie Diet for Weight Gain

Total Daily Calories: 3000–3500 kcal (adjust based on activity level)

This plan incorporates calorie-dense foods with balanced macronutrients to promote healthy weight gain.
________________________________________
Breakfast
•Non-Vegetarian Option:
    o	3 scrambled eggs cooked in 1 tsp butter with mushrooms and spinach
    o	2 slices whole-grain toast with peanut butter
    o	1 cup whole milk or a high-calorie milk alternative
    o	Approximate Calories: 800–1000 kcal
•Vegetarian Option:
    o	Smoothie: 1 banana, 1/2 cup Greek yogurt, 1/2 cup almond milk, 1 tbsp almond butter, 1 tsp honey, and 1 scoop protein powder
    o	1 slice whole-grain toast with cream cheese
    o	Approximate Calories: 800–1000 kcal
________________________________________
Lunch
•Non-Vegetarian Option:
    o	Grilled chicken thighs (6 oz)
    o	1 cup cooked quinoa or couscous
    o	1 cup roasted sweet potato cubes
    o	1 cup sautéed green beans with 1 tsp olive oil
    o	Approximate Calories: 800–1100 kcal
•Vegetarian Option:
    o	Paneer tikka (6 oz) or chickpea curry (1 cup)
    o	1 cup cooked quinoa or couscous
    o	1 cup roasted sweet potato cubes
    o	1 cup sautéed green beans with 1 tsp olive oil
    o	Approximate Calories: 800–1100 kcal
________________________________________
Dinner
•Non-Vegetarian Option:
    o	Baked salmon (6 oz) or grilled turkey breast (6 oz)
    o	1 large baked potato topped with 1 tbsp sour cream and chives
    o	1 cup sautéed mixed vegetables with 1 tsp olive oil
    o	Approximate Calories: 800–1000 kcal
•Vegetarian Option:
    o	Grilled tofu (6 oz) or lentil stew (1 cup)
    o	1 large baked potato topped with 1 tbsp sour cream and chives
    o	1 cup sautéed mixed vegetables with 1 tsp olive oil
    o	Approximate Calories: 800–1000 kcal
