Balanced Calorie Diet for Weight Loss

Total Daily Calories: 2000–2500 kcal (adjust based on individual needs)

This plan ensures balanced macronutrients, prioritizing protein and fiber to maintain satiety while promoting fat loss.
________________________________________
Breakfast
•Non-Vegetarian Option:
    o	2 boiled eggs or scrambled eggs (3 egg whites, 1 whole egg)
    o	1 slice whole-grain toast with avocado (1/4 avocado)
    o	1 medium orange or apple
    o	1 cup black coffee or unsweetened tea
    o	Approximate Calories: 400–500 kcal
•Vegetarian Option:
    o	Smoothie: 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk, 1/2 cup Greek yogurt, 1/2 cup berries, 1 tsp chia seeds, and 1 tbsp protein powder (plant-based)
    o	1 slice whole-grain toast with almond butter
    o	Approximate Calories: 400–500 kcal
________________________________________
Lunch
•Non-Vegetarian Option:
    o	Grilled chicken breast (6 oz)
    o	1 cup cooked quinoa or brown rice
    o	1 cup steamed broccoli or spinach
    o	1 tsp olive oil or lemon-tahini dressing
    o	Approximate Calories: 600–700 kcal
•Vegetarian Option:
    o	Grilled tofu (6 oz) or lentil salad (1 cup cooked lentils, mixed greens, 1 tsp olive oil)
    o	1 cup cooked quinoa or brown rice
    o	1 cup steamed broccoli or spinach
    o	Approximate Calories: 600–700 kcal
________________________________________
Dinner
•Non-Vegetarian Option:
    o	Grilled turkey breast (6 oz) or baked salmon (6 oz)
    o	1 medium baked sweet potato
    o	1 cup sautéed green beans with 1 tsp olive oil
    o	Approximate Calories: 500–600 kcal
•Vegetarian Option:
    o	Grilled paneer (6 oz) or chickpea curry (1 cup)
    o	1 medium baked sweet potato
    o	1 cup sautéed green beans with 1 tsp olive oil
    o	Approximate Calories: 500–600 kcal
