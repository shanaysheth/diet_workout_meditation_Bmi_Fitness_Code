Balanced Calorie Diet for Toning

Total Daily Calories: 2500–3000 kcal (adjust based on activity level)

This plan focuses on balanced meals with adequate protein and complex carbohydrates for muscle repair and recovery.
________________________________________
Breakfast
•Non-Vegetarian Option:
    o	2 scrambled eggs with spinach and tomatoes
    o	2 slices whole-grain toast with butter or avocado spread
    o	1 medium banana or apple
    o	1 cup whole milk or almond milk
    o	Approximate Calories: 600–700 kcal
•Vegetarian Option:
    o	Oats with 1/2 cup almond milk, 1 tbsp peanut butter, 1 tsp chia seeds, and 1/2 cup berries
    o	1 slice whole-grain toast with almond butter
    o	Approximate Calories: 600–700 kcal
________________________________________
Lunch
•Non-Vegetarian Option:
    o	Grilled chicken thighs (6 oz)
    o	1 cup cooked brown rice
    o	1 cup roasted vegetables (e.g., zucchini, carrots, bell peppers) with 1 tsp olive oil
    o	Approximate Calories: 700–800 kcal
•Vegetarian Option:
    o	Grilled paneer (6 oz) or chickpea salad (1 cup chickpeas, mixed greens, 1 tsp olive oil)
    o	1 cup cooked brown rice
    o	1 cup roasted vegetables (e.g., zucchini, carrots, bell peppers)
    o	Approximate Calories: 700–800 kcal
________________________________________
Dinner
•Non-Vegetarian Option:
    o	Baked salmon (6 oz) or lean beef steak (6 oz)
    o	1 large baked potato topped with 1 tbsp Greek yogurt
    o	1 cup sautéed spinach with garlic and 1 tsp olive oil
    o	Approximate Calories: 700–800 kcal
•Vegetarian Option:
    o	Grilled tempeh (6 oz) or lentil stew (1 cup)
    o	1 large baked potato topped with 1 tbsp Greek yogurt
    o	1 cup sautéed spinach with garlic and 1 tsp olive oil
    o	Approximate Calories: 700–800 kcal
