
Moderate-Intensity Weight Loss Workout
Total Time: 20 Minutes
Objective: Improve endurance, burn calories, and build strength at a sustainable pace.
________________________________________
Warm-Up (3 Minutes)
1.	Marching in Place: 1 minute
2.	Arm Circles (forward and backward): 1 minute
3.	Bodyweight Squats: 1 minute
________________________________________
Main Workout (15 Minutes, Circuit Style)
Perform each exercise for 40 seconds, rest for 20 seconds. Complete 2 rounds.
1.	Step-Back Lunges
    o	Step one leg back into a lunge position, alternate legs.
    o	Works the glutes and quads with controlled movements.
2.	Knee Push-Ups
    o	Modified push-ups with knees on the ground.
    o	Builds upper body strength at a manageable intensity.
3.	Standing Side Crunches
    o	Bring one elbow to the side of the knee while lifting the leg, alternating sides.
    o	Engages the obliques and improves balance.
4.	Glute Bridges
    o	Lie on your back, feet flat on the ground, and lift your hips towards the ceiling.
    o	Strengthens the core and glutes.
5.	Side-to-Side Steps
    o	Step side-to-side in a slight squat position.
    o	Low-impact cardio and leg engagement.
6.	Plank Hold (Knees or Toes)
    o	Hold a plank position on forearms, keeping the back straight.
    o	Improves core strength.
7.	Low-Impact Jumping Jacks
    o	Step one leg out to the side as arms raise overhead, alternating sides.
    o	Gentle cardio option for full-body movement.
8.	Seated Leg Lifts
    o	Sit on the floor, leaning slightly back, and lift legs alternately.
    o	Targets the lower abs.
________________________________________
Cool Down (2 Minutes)
1.	Hamstring Stretch: 1 minute
2.	Cat-Cow Stretch: 1 minute
