
Low-Calorie Diet for Toning

Total Daily Calories: 1500–1800 kcal (adjust based on activity level)

This plan emphasizes lean protein and moderate carbohydrates for muscle repair and toning.
________________________________________
Breakfast
•Non-Vegetarian Option:
    o	1 scrambled egg with 1/2 cup sautéed spinach
    o	1 slice whole-grain toast with a light spread of butter
    o	1 medium orange
    o	Approximate Calories: 300–350 kcal
•Vegetarian Option:
    o	Overnight oats: 1/4 cup oats, 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk, 1/4 cup Greek yogurt, and 1/2 tbsp honey
    o	Approximate Calories: 300–350 kcal
________________________________________
Lunch
•Non-Vegetarian Option:
    o	Grilled chicken breast (5 oz)
    o	1/2 cup cooked quinoa or couscous
    o	1 cup roasted or steamed green beans
    o	1 tsp olive oil for dressing
    o	Approximate Calories: 400–500 kcal
•Vegetarian Option:
    o	Grilled tofu (5 oz) or chickpea salad (1/2 cup chickpeas, 1 cup mixed greens)
    o	1/2 cup cooked quinoa or couscous
    o	1 cup roasted or steamed green beans
    o	Approximate Calories: 400–500 kcal
________________________________________
Dinner
•Non-Vegetarian Option:
    o	Baked fish (5 oz)
    o	1 cup steamed spinach or zucchini
    o	1/2 cup mashed sweet potato with a sprinkle of cinnamon
    o	Approximate Calories: 400–500 kcal
•Vegetarian Option:
    o	Grilled tempeh (5 oz) or lentil soup (1 cup)
    o	1 cup steamed spinach or zucchini
    o	1/2 cup mashed sweet potato with a sprinkle of cinnamon
    o	Approximate Calories: 400–500 kcal
