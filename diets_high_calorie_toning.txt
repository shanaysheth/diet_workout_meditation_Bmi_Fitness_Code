High-Calorie Diet for Toning:

Total Daily Calories: 2500–3000 kcal (adjust based on activity level and individual goals)

This plan emphasizes protein for muscle repair, healthy fats for energy, and complex carbs for sustained performance.
________________________________________
Breakfast
•Non-Vegetarian Option:
    o	Omelette (3 whole eggs, 2 egg whites) with spinach, tomatoes, and 1 tbsp feta cheese
    o	2 slices whole-grain toast with avocado spread (1/2 avocado)
    o	1 cup mixed berries
    o	1 cup whole milk or unsweetened almond milk
    o	Approximate Calories: 700–850 kcal
•Vegetarian Option:
    o	Smoothie bowl: Blend 1 frozen banana, 1/2 cup Greek yogurt, 1/2 cup almond milk, and 1 scoop plant-based protein powder; top with granola (1/4 cup), chia seeds (1 tsp), and sliced fruit
    o	1 slice whole-grain toast with almond butter
    o	Approximate Calories: 700–850 kcal
________________________________________
Lunch
•Non-Vegetarian Option:
    o	Grilled chicken breast (6 oz)
    o	1 cup cooked quinoa or brown rice
    o	1 cup steamed green beans
    o	1 tbsp olive oil and lemon dressing
    o	1/2 avocado
    o   Approximate Calories: 750–900 kcal
•Vegetarian Option:
    o	Grilled tempeh (6 oz) or black beans (1/2 cup)
    o	1 cup cooked quinoa or brown rice
    o	1 cup steamed green beans
    o	1 tbsp olive oil and lemon dressing
    o	1/2 avocado
    o	Approximate Calories: 750–900 kcal
