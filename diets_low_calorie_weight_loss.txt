
Low-Calorie Diet for Weight Loss

Total Daily Calories: 1200–1500 kcal (adjust based on individual needs)

This plan prioritizes nutrient-dense, low-calorie foods to create a calorie deficit while providing essential nutrients.
________________________________________
Breakfast
•Non-Vegetarian Option:
    o	1 boiled egg or poached egg
    o	1 slice whole-grain toast with 1/4 avocado
    o	1 medium apple or a handful of berries
    o	Approximate Calories: 250–300 kcal
•Vegetarian Option:
    o	Smoothie: 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk, 1/2 cup spinach, 1/2 banana, and 1 tbsp chia seeds
    o	Approximate Calories: 250–300 kcal
________________________________________
Lunch
•Non-Vegetarian Option:
    o	Grilled chicken breast (4 oz)
    o	1 cup steamed broccoli or spinach
    o	1/2 cup cooked quinoa or brown rice
    o	1 tsp olive oil for dressing
    o	Approximate Calories: 350–400 kcal
•Vegetarian Option:
    o	Grilled tofu (4 oz) or chickpea salad (1/2 cup chickpeas, mixed greens, 1 tsp olive oil)
    o	1 cup steamed broccoli or spinach
    o	1/2 cup cooked quinoa or brown rice
    o	Approximate Calories: 350–400 kcal
________________________________________
Dinner
•Non-Vegetarian Option:
    o	Baked fish (4 oz) or turkey breast
    o	1 cup steamed zucchini or mixed veggies
    o	1/2 medium baked sweet potato
    o	Approximate Calories: 350–400 kcal
•Vegetarian Option:
    o	Grilled paneer (4 oz) or lentil soup (1 cup)
    o	1 cup steamed zucchini or mixed veggies
    o	1/2 medium baked sweet potato
    o	Approximate Calories: 350–400 kcal
